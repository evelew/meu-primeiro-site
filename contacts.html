WEBSTORES HELP & CUSTOMER SERVICES MAIN WEBSTORE

For help and support on all main webstore orders (i.e. all orders except those made on the separate Jewellery store) and to contact the main webstore Customer Services team, please go to the main webstore's HELP & SUPPORT CENTRE >

There you can look for an answer to your question in the Frequently Asked Questions. If you cannot find the answer to your question you can contact customer services via the Contact Us link at the bottom of any page.

The customer services team endeavor to respond to all queries within 2 working days (Mon-Fri).
JEWELLERY STORE
The Jewellery store is a separately run store from the main webstore. The Customer Services team that reply to the contact form from the main webstore Help & Support Centre are therefore only able to help with orders from the main store. For help with orders from the Jewellery store and to contact the Jewellery store Customer Services team, please go to the JEWELLERY HELP PAGE
